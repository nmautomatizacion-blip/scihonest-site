About SciHonest

SciHonest is run by Mara Ruzic (Zagreb, Croatia). She spent eight years at Jutarnji list covering science and health, then left in 2022 to focus on scholarly publishing integrity. Her reporting has been cited by Retraction Watch and Science|Business.

Our editorial policy is simple: no anonymous sources without a second corroboration; no payments for information; and no publication of names of individual affiliate authors who are themselves victims of paper mills.
